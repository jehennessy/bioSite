My Story

To me riding is not just a hobby, its a lifestyle. Riding bikes has brought amazing stories, people, and places into my life. With over 40 years of experience riding, starting off with racing dirt bikes as a youngster, I want to take it to a new level. At 55 years young I want to reinvent myself. I want to race in the super-sport category at racetracks across America. I have many experiences in my life but being on a bike is the most humbling thing, it reminds me I'm human, it gives me energy, and it fills my life with purpose.

Wounded Warrior Project

I currently have the opportunity to take my dreams into full action. BMW has given me the offer to join their school of racing. This training will give me a racing license that I will be able to utilize in the race for the Wounded Warrior Project. If you have the means to help me in my cause and would like to help support my project, you can strengthen my fight on my Go Fund Me page.
